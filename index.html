Connecticut

Connecticut is a small state in Northeast USA. It is lovely, somewhat rural, between major cities, Boston and New York. In CT I am near nature and wildlife, the beach, and U.S. historical sites. I never imagined moving here from Utah, which I thought was the "Wild West", that I would see so much wildlife, even just in my own yard! Foxes, hawks, bears, coyotes, bobcats, rabbits, squirrels, chipmunks, and even bears share the forest with us.
