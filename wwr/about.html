History

Downstream Driftwood Rafting Company began as a way to supply LDS wards with resources for memorable outdoor adventures for their youth activities and camps. What began as a local volunteer project has now expanded into a service supplying wards all throughout the western states with transportation, guides, and gear for amazing and safe youth group adventures.
